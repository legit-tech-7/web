What is LitGet?

LitGet is a fully licensed digital platform specializing in cryptocurrency trading and blockchain solutions. Our headquarters are located at 71-75 Shelton Street, Covent Garden, London, United Kingdom, WC2H 9JQ, and we operate in compliance with international regulations.

What services does LitGet offer?

We provide:

  • Secure crypto/fiat transactions
  • AI-powered portfolio tracking
  • Multi-chain wallet integration
  • Institutional-grade trading tools
Who can use LitGet?

Our platform is available to:

  • Individuals aged 18+ (with KYC verification)
  • Businesses (corporate accounts available)
  • Institutions (custom API solutions)

Note: Excluded jurisdictions apply due to regulatory requirements.

How do I register?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Visit LitGet.com/signup
  2. Submit your email and create a password
  3. Complete identity verification (KYC)
  4. Fund your account and start trading
Are there fees for registration?

Account creation is 100% free. Transaction fees apply only when you:

  • Deposit/withdraw funds (0.1%-2.5%)
  • Trade assets (0.05%-0.25% per trade)

View our full Fee Schedule for details.

I'm having registration issues. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Use recommended browsers (Chrome or Firefox)
  3. Check your spam folder for verification emails
  4. Disable any ad-blockers or VPNs temporarily

If issues persist, contact our support team at support@litget.com with screenshots of the problem.
